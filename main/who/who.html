So Who Are These Guys?

Carlos → President

Originally from Guayaquil, Ecuador, Carlos began smoking cigarettes at the age of 17. With concerns for his health and being around for his children Carlos began vaping in June of 2013. Disgusted with the current offerings on the market and the high prices for "good juice" Carlos began to set the blue prints for 7 Crimes in June 2014.

Flavor profile includes: Summer fruits (no citrus), RY4 & Tobaccos and Bakery flavors.

Justin → Marketing & R&D

A Charleston, SC native. Justin began smoking at the age of 15. Just like Carlos Justin decided to try vaping in March of 2014 along with James after a long discussion about life, kids, death, etc etc over some fine craft beers. 3 hours and only D.D. Peckers knows how many beers, Justin and James risked their lives to run across Savannah Highway during rush hour traffic in West Ashley to a local vape shop to check it out and never looked back.

Flavor profile includes: Sweets, Bakery Flavors and Creams
